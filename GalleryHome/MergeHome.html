Our History

UCL Rocket is the most successful team under UCL Racing, having been formed in September of 2021, towards the end of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Despite only having run for two years and with the struggles from COVID, we have participated and achieved excellent results in competitions both nationally and internationally!

We won the UK’s National Rocketry Championship back to back , became one of the only student led teams to hot-fire a liquid engine, and placed second in Europe at the spaceport america cup; beating out over 100 global contenders. including Duke and Berkley. We also have the highest amount of Redbull and Aerospace graduates out of any other UCLR team.

Excelsior

Our Excelsior liquid engine team is unwaveringly committed to spearheading our most extensive and ambitious projects, developing the uk’s first refractory and regeneratively cooled liquid engine . Having hot fired our Meliora engine to 730N in our first year, we at Excelsior have not only met but far exceeded all expectations.

Now, picture us aiming even higher—we're set on being the first European student team to reach space with a bi-propellant liquid rocket engine. As we gear up for Race2Space25, our record breaking ambitions remain steadfast as we continue to redefine the bounds student-led space exploration.

Spaceport America Cup

At UCL Rocket, our relentless pursuit of excellence drives us to push boundaries and redefine what's possible. After proudly securing second place out of 150 teams among European teams , we’re more determined than ever to return to America stronger. This time, we aim to test cutting-edge systems critical to advancing our high-altitude capabilities, with our sights set on shattering the UK altitude record.

Our team's vision extends far beyond the present. We’re actively developing a groundbreaking spaceshot rocket , with the goal of becoming the first European team to reach space. With innovations like our custom-built , in-house GPS system b> and a meticulously designed dual deployment recovery mechanism, we are setting new standards in rocketry. The future of space exploration is within our grasp, and we’re ready to make history.

First Year Team

At UCL Rocket, we prioritize hands-on learning, embodied by our first year competition team, comprising solely First-Year Engineers. From the outset, these students tackle the challenge of manufacturing and competing with their own unique rocket designs. This team is also fully led by our first years allowing them to make critical budgetary choices from the onset and joining our Board of Directors.

Despite their limited experience, our first year teams have consistently exceeds expectations, triumphing over senior teams and winning the National Rocketry Championship twice in a row!

S.T.A.R.S

Our Specialised Training and Rocketry Skills programme is our latest addition to UCL Rocket. The S.T.A.R.S program, in collaboration with UKRA, offers three certifications, including two for High Power Rocketry and one for Model Rocketry. Through this program, we aim to provide certifications to all aspiring engineers who wish to launch their own amateur rockets.

S.T.A.R.S not only enhances technical expertise but also sparks interest to people of all academic levels through multiple launches, hands-on experiences, and creative freedom, reinforcing our commitment to a dynamic UCL Rocket community.
