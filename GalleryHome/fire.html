Our History

At UCL Rocket, we prioritize hands-on learning, embodied by our F.I.R.E competition team, comprising solely First-Year Engineers. From the outset, these students tackle the challenge of manufacturing and competing with their own unique rocket designs.

Despite their limited experience, the F.I.R.E team consistently exceeds expectations, triumphing over senior teams and winning the National Rocketry Championship. Currently, F.I.R.E is focused on advancing their Solid motor technology, with the goal of breaching the Kármán line in the coming years!
